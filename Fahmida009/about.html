About IUT

Islamic University of Technology (IUT) is a subsidiary organ of the Organisation of Islamic Cooperation (OIC). It is located in Gazipur, Bangladesh, and offers education and research opportunities in engineering, technology, and technical education.

Main Objectives

  • Develop human resources of the OIC member states.
  • Offer advanced engineering and technical education.
  • Enhance research and innovation in applied sciences.
  • Promote Islamic values through modern education.
