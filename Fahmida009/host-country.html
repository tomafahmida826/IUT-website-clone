Host Country Students

The Islamic University of Technology (IUT), while primarily serving students from OIC member states, also admits students from the host country, Bangladesh. These students are selected through a competitive national admission process and benefit from the university’s international standards and multicultural environment.

Host country students have been making remarkable contributions in various academic, co-curricular, and extracurricular activities. They regularly participate in international competitions, research projects, and industry collaborations, upholding IUT’s commitment to excellence.

Opportunities for Host Country Students:

  • Full access to IUT’s academic programs in engineering, science, and technology
  • Participation in international exchange and internship programs
  • Engagement in student-led clubs, societies, and innovation hubs
  • Scholarships based on merit and need

Achievements

Bangladeshi students have consistently ranked at the top of their academic programs, won international competitions, and represented IUT in global forums, contributing to the university’s outstanding reputation.
