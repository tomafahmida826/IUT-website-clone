International Students

As a subsidiary organ of the Organisation of Islamic Cooperation (OIC), the Islamic University of Technology (IUT) is committed to fostering international cooperation in education. IUT admits students from OIC member states, offering them access to high-quality education in engineering, science, and technology.

Diverse Student Body

IUT hosts students from over 40 OIC member countries, creating a truly multicultural academic environment. This diversity promotes intercultural understanding, collaboration, and lasting friendships among students from across Asia, Africa, and the Middle East.

Admission and Scholarships

  • Students are nominated by their respective governments through OIC.
  • Admitted international students receive full or partial scholarships, including tuition, accommodation, and stipends.
  • IUT provides a smooth onboarding process, including visa support, campus orientation, and international student support services.

Student Life

International students engage in various academic and cultural events, international conferences, leadership programs, and innovation challenges. Dedicated dormitories, halal dining, multilingual faculty, and support staff ensure a welcoming environment.
