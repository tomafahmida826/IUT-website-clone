Networking Research Group

Prof. Dr. Muhammad Mahbub Alam
Professor, CSE Department
Leader

The Networking Research Group focuses on protocols for wired and wireless networks, especially wireless sensor networks, mobile ad hoc networks, and more.

Systems and Software Lab (SSL)

Dr. Md. Kamrul Hasan
Professor, CSE Department
Leader

SSL develops software for gesture recognition, speech-based text correction, and more. It includes human-computer interaction, AI, and robotics research.

Concrete Lab

Dr. Md. Tarek Uddin
Professor, CEE Department
Leader

This lab focuses on construction material durability and sustainability. Research includes the use of slag and other materials in concrete.

Geotechnical Lab

Dr. Shakil Mohammad Rifat
Professor, CEE Department
Leader

This lab investigates properties of soil and its behavior under various load conditions, including seismic, slope stability, and tunnel engineering.

Transportation Research Lab

Dr. Hossain Md. Shahian
Professor, CEE Department
Leader

Focuses on road safety, traffic flow theory, transportation planning, and simulation of urban transport systems.

Language Lab

Dr. Md. Shahadat Hossain Khan
Professor, TVE Department
Leader

The lab supports students from different OIC countries with spoken English and foreign language training in Arabic and French.
